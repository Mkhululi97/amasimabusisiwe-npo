About Us

The purpose of this foundation is to assit children with school material and resources. Better the lives of people living in disadvantage members of the community at large with food parcels and clothing.

Our services

Feeding schemes at clinics (offering the elderly food in the morning so they cant take their medication). Soup kitchens, and food parcels giving fruits to the people that collect waste and refuge.

Personal hygiene issuing out sanitary towels for girls in schools, donating school shoes to learns.
